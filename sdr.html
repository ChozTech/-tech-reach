💼 Sales Development Representative (SDR)

📘 Overview

SDRs handle outbound prospecting and set up meetings for AEs. It’s the most common entry point into SaaS sales.

💰 Median Earnings

  • Base: $55,000
  • OTE: $75,000
  • Top: $100,000+

🚀 How to Break In

No degree needed. What matters: grit, cold outreach, LinkedIn presence, coachability, and consistency.

✉️ Cold Outreach Templates

Email: Hi [Name], I admire your work at [Company]. I’m aiming to break into tech sales and would be grateful to connect for a quick chat.

Phone: Hey [Name], I’ll be quick — I’m exploring SaaS and think your journey is incredible. Mind if I ask a quick question?

🏢 Top Companies Hiring SDRs

  • Salesforce
  • Gong
  • Outreach.io
  • HubSpot
  • Oracle

🎓 Internships & Programs

Check out: Oracle Sales Academy, HubSpot Early Careers, LinkedIn BDR program. DM reps and recruiters directly to stand out.
